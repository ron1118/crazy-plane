About Crazy Plane

Crazy Plane is an exhilarating online flight simulation game that puts you in control of a high-performance aircraft. Navigate through challenging environments, complete daring missions, and test your piloting skills in this action-packed adventure.

With realistic physics and intuitive controls, Crazy Plane offers an immersive experience for both casual players and flight simulation enthusiasts. The game features multiple levels of increasing difficulty, various aircraft to unlock, and stunning environments to explore.

Game Features

Realistic Physics

Experience authentic flight dynamics with our advanced physics engine that simulates real-world aerodynamics.

Multiple Aircraft

Unlock and pilot different planes, each with unique handling characteristics and performance specs.

Challenging Missions

Complete various objectives from precision landings to aerial combat scenarios and rescue operations.

Stunning Environments

Fly through diverse landscapes including cities, mountains, and islands with beautiful graphics.

Leaderboards

Mobile Friendly

How to Play

Mastering Crazy Plane requires skill and precision. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Use gradual throttle adjustments for smooth takeoffs and landings
  • Monitor your altitude carefully to avoid collisions with terrain
  • Complete bonus objectives to earn extra points and unlock new aircraft
  • Practice in the training mode before attempting difficult missions
  • Watch your fuel gauge and plan your route efficiently

The game saves your progress automatically, so you can pick up where you left off anytime.
